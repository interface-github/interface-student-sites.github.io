About

Cinque Terre

I live to give and travel! In the last three years I've visted 12 different countries and I inspire others to travel. The amount one can grow from one trip is priceless.

I reside in the wonderful Village of Kennard, population 370. This village is made up of families and friends looking out for each other on a daily basis. Everyone waves and it is a great place to be.

Proud Omaha native and I feel blessed to have grown up in such an amazing city.

I'm married to a wonderful tattoo artist who impresses me daily with his work and his loving support.

Very excited and stoked about learning, and super passionate about teaching.

I truly believe that the more you GIVE the more you LIVE.
