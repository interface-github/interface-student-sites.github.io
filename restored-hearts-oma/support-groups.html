Support Groups

Seeds of Hope

If your past includes sexual abuse that continues to creep into present life and relationships, there is hope. This group for women is a safe place to explore the wounds of the past, and how to find freedom for the present. The booklet "When Trust Is Lost" by Dr. Dan Allendar, is used as a springboard for discussion.

Contact us for group locations & times

This 12-week DVD series is a more intense focus - joining with others and with God to continue processing and healing the damage from past sexual abuse events. Limited group size. Contact us for group locations & times
